Why BCA ?

Graduates of BCA programs are eligible to apply for positions as system analysts, managers, project managers,
database administrators, designers, and programmers in IT firms. Manufacturing and information businesses are
constantly looking for BCA graduates.

Students who have earned a bachelor's degree in computer applications are also qualified to enroll in any programs
offered by the School of Management and Information Technology, including the MCA, MIT, MBA, and many more

Why BCA at Padmashree ?

In order for our young aspirants to become successful IT managers and programmers in the future, Padmashree recognizes
the importance of IT management studies and seeks to develop its basis. The BCA program at Padmashree specifically works
in the following areas to achieve this admirable goal:

Internships in different IT and IT management companies.

Projects include seminars and other IT-related initiatives.

Outstanding educators with a proven track record of achievement and work experience.

Well-equipped computer and digital laboratories.

Frequent assessments and prompt feedback.

Support with placement through career counseling.
