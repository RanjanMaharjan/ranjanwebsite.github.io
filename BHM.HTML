BABHM at Padmashree

You will learn important business and management concepts as well as acquire information, traits,
abilities, and experience in important hospitality-related areas during this course. You can select from
a variety of electives to concentrate on the subject you are enthusiastic about.

Eligibility

Pass Grade XII with minimum of 45% in 2 subjects.
Minimum of 45% in English in Grade XII.
Note: English can also count as 1 subject in 1 above.

An Extra Edge in IT at Padmashree

Padmashree equips you with the entrepreneurial
mindset and creative problem-solving abilities needed to develop businesses in the field.

The BHM at Padmashree follows the trimester system,
with nine semesters spread over three years and the final tenth semester is separated for the internship.
Because of this, despite having a rigorous curriculum, graduates will be more prepared for the workforce than
those from other institutions.

Padmashree is constantly prepared to assist students
in honing their skills through continuous seminars and guest lectures from experts in the field.

Reputable organizations provide internationally recognized
certificates through prepared exams and related training. They fill the gap between the prescribed curriculum and the
hospitality sector that is always evolving.

Padmashree believes in collaborations with well-known names
in the hospitality business for collaborative and applied research, information sharing, and most crucially, the transfer of
practical skills to our students.
